3. Android version and security patch date?

4. Screen: IPS or TN? Touch sampling rate for stylus?

5. Battery capacity (mAh) and real battery life (video/idle)?

6. Does it support USB OTG?

7. MicroSD max capacity?

8. Warranty: 1 year? Who pays return shipping for DOA units?

9. Can you pre-install our APK (kiosk mode) before shipping? Cost per unit?

10. MOQ for custom boot logo / box with "SeNote" branding?

11. Provide 2-minute factory video of EXACT unit we will receive.

12. Lead time for 30 / 100 / 200 units?

13. Sea freight quote to Dakar, Senegal — CIF or FOB Shenzhen?
